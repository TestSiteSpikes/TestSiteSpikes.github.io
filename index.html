Our Story

Welcome

Spike's Pub & Grub is the place to have a beer and cocktail with friends and family while enjoying some great fish, pork, and burgers.

We have a twist on American Pub Food. We will have burgers, pork loin, fish, nachos, hotdogs, chili, salad, nachos, calzones, and whatever else strikes our guests fancy.
